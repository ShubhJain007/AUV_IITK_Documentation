/provider_dvl/pd0_packet

The `pd0_packet topics contains all the informations of the DVL. It is being sent from the DVL at every signals.

The specifications of the PD0 packets can be found on the Teledyne Documentation (c.f. Github). It is composed of the other messages of the DVL and does not contain any raw data. The specifications for all the other messages can also be found on the documentation.
